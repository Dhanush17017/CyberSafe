Cyber Tips

Use Strong Passwords

Always use complex passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

Think Before You Click

Avoid suspicious links and attachments in emails or SMS.

Update Software Regularly

Keep your devices and apps updated to avoid security vulnerabilities.

Use Secure Wi-Fi

Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive logins. Use VPN when possible.
